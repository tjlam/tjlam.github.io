Medical Facials

At MD Laser Skin Clinic, we keep our facials simple and effective... no fluff and stuff or over-promising results, and we will not “push you” to buy products. We are dedicated to caring for your skin and will recommend treatments and products only if we think you will benefit and if you are interested. Otherwise, just relax, and enjoy the benefits of the science of medical facials to improve or maintain your skin at the expertise of our experienced medical aesthetician, and be sure to wear a good sunscreen to protect the results!

Below is a list of the simple medical facials that we suggest for all skin types:



Deep Cleansing Facial - $85

This basic facial is our most popular cleansing treatment for oily skin, acne-prone skin, skin with large pores and comodones. It is suitable for all skin types. The skin is cleansed, gently exfoliated, removing toxins and impurities through manual and chemical procedures. A mixture of botanical extracts and vitamins are applied to hydrate and nourish your skin. For an extra $25, we will apply our ExCeliience Thermo-Sculpt mask that will firm and tone your skin to look more radiant and rejuvenated. Try this facial in combination with our Anti-aging Eye Care treatment (for $30 extra) to get that super-refreshed look!


Anti-aging Eye Care Treatment - $30

This professional treatment uses specially selected biologically active formulation that help reduce the signs of aging around the eyes – fine lines, wrinkles, dark circles and puffiness. We recommend this treatment in combination with the Deep Cleansing Facial to improve your overall skin tone, or with Botox Cosmetic (consultation required with our doctor), if you have those really deep, age-telling “crow’s feet” at your eyes when you smile.


Microdermabrasion Facial - $85

Microdermabrasion is a very popular stimulating skin procedure that exfoliates using mild suction and a micro-crystalline spray to mechanically remove dull, surface dead skin and impurities within the pores, great for acneic skin (non-cystic type). The result is a smoother textured skin, with younger and softer, brighter skin. We recommend this procedure followed by a customized nourishing mask treatment or anti-aging /skin lightening serum treatment for only $25 extra.
