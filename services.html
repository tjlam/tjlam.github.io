Laser IPL Hair Removal

Laser hair removal is a process that safely eliminates unwanted body hair by using various wavelengths of laser light that is selectively absorbed by the darker pigment in hair follicles. The latest technology uses Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) to deliver light energy into the follicle which is transformed into heat that destroys the hair bulb, leaving the surrounding skin unharmed. Cooling of the skin is done through the application of ice packs, cooling gels and sprays, making the procedure virtually painfree, without the need for expensive topical anaesthetic creams.
Most treatments take only minutes to under an hour to administer, depending on the size of the body area. Your skin must be protected from sun exposure or chemicals that can irritate the treatment site for approximately one week. Therefore, a good sunscreen is recommended. The number of treatments will depend on the body part, the hair density and the type of skin you have. The results will speak for themselves - smooth, silky, stubble-free, healthy looking skin, free of infections, ingrown hairs, redness and hair "shadows".
