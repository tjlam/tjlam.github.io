Our Staff

Dr. Kenneth Lam

Dr. Kenneth Lam graduated from the Faculty of Medicine, University of Toronto in 1987. He has been a general practitioner in Family Medicine for over 20 years, and currently owns and works in two busy medical practices in Toronto. He is also the medical director of MD Laser Skin Clinic, which opened in 2005. Dr. Lam is an examiner for the Medical Council of Canada, which is the licensing board for all new Canadian doctors. He is also a certified injector for BOTOX COSMETIC®, and cosmetic fillers. Dr. Lam has taken numerous courses in Aesthetic Medicine, and updates his techniques and skills though continuing education. "No one wants to look old or feel old prematurely, so I opened the MD Laser Skin Clinic in partnership with my wife (see below), with the goal of providing anti-aging laser and facial treatments to our clients." Dr. Lam is the father of two teenage children. During his spare time, he enjoys playing tennis, golf, and gardening.

Dr. Lily Lo

Dr. Lily Lo graduated from the Faculty of Dentistry, University of Toronto, in 1990, where she is also a part-time clinical instructor. Since then, Dr. Lo is a general dentist and currently owns two dental practices- located in Toronto and Richmond Hill with a focus on Cosmetic Dentistry. As well, she has taken advanced education in the field of orthodontics, and thus, has successfully created beautiful smiles for children, teens and adults for over 20 years. Dr. Lo is also a certified provider of the Invisalign "braceless" system - a great alternative for adults who have mild to moderately malaligned teeth. In 2005, Dr. Lo opened the MD Laser Skin Clinic with her husband, Dr. Kenneth Lam. She was certified in 2007 to perform BOTOX COSMETIC® and injectable fillers under Dr. Lam's delegation. Dr. Lo is also a provider of BOTOX COSMETIC® Therapeutic which can be used for treatment of TMJD (Temporal Mandibular Joint Dysfunction) muscle hyperactivity and pain. "I have always been fascinated by the uniqueness and beauty of the human face. As a cosmetic dentist who has been shaping smiles for years using veneers, orthodontics, crowns and implants, I evaluate not only teeth, but all elements of facial anatomy including the lips, chin, cheeks, nose and forehead. I am thrilled to be able to perform procedures that not only give patients a fantastic smile, but also rejuvenate the skin for a natural, healthy, younger looking face!"

Eva Ho

Eva Ho began her spa aesthetician career in 1999. In the year of 2000, she also received advanced aesthetics and medical aesthetics training, and then becoming a certified medical aesthetician in 2001. She has been a full time Medical Laser Technician in various cosmetic clinics and medical spa since 2003. In 2006, Eva was employed as a clinical doctor's assistant in a dermatologist clinic, performing all laser related treatments to patients. Her extensive medical laser experience has led her to become a senior laser technician in MD Laser Skin Clinic. In her spare time, Eva continuously updates herself by taking related courses and attending workshops to learn about the latest cosmetic laser technology. Her areas of expertise include but not limited to laser hair removal, laser skin rejuvenation, laser skin tightening, fractional laser resurfacing, microdermabrasion, chemical peels and medical facials. In her spare time, she enjoys shopping (what else?)

Olive Amson

Olive Amson has been a certified aesthetician performing all aspects of beauty treatment since the early 1970's. In 2001, Olive received her training in laser aesthetics, primarily in laser hair removal and photo-rejuvenation. She worked as a laser technician for the next several years in various cosmetic surgical clinics owned by plastic surgeons and specialists. Her expertise led her to start a consulting business for setting up laser clinics for clients in the Greater Toronto area. In 2003, she started her own laser spa in Richmond Hill and now has a well-established laser clinic located at 16th Ave Dental & Laser Medi-spa. Olive currently offers her services as the Laser Director for the new Fractional Technology at MD Laser Skin Clinic, a laser spa that she helped set up in 2005. Welcome back, Olive!
