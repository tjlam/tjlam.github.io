About Us

Conveniently located at the Don Valley Parkway and Lawrence Ave. East, MD Laser Skin Clinic is a cosmetic laser medispa owned and operated by healthcare professionals dedicated to preserving health and beauty to the skin, without surgery. In operation since 2005, our clinic offers a variety of anti-aging treatments that have a long safety track record and efficacy – BOTOX cosmetic® and therapeutic, Juvederm fillers, laser hair removal, IPL photorejuvenation, micro-needling, medical peels and much more.

Our laser and medical aestheticians are highly knowledgeable and are extremely experienced. We take great pride in our expertise in skin science and getting to know our clients individually to address their concerns. Our fees are competitive without sacrificing service and quality care. All of our cosmetic injections are performed by Dr. Ken Lam, MD, or Dr. Lily Lo, D.D.S.

For more information, please book a personal consultation with one of our staff to find out what solutions are best for you. Call 416-445-7546 (SKIN) today!

Meet Our Staff

dr. kenneth lam

Dr. Lam graduated from the University of Toronto, Faculty of Medicine, in 1987. He has enjoyed being a general practitioner in Family Medicine works in his two busy medical practices in Toronto for many years. He is also an examiner for the Medical Council of Canada, which is the licensing board for all new doctors from other countries. In 2005, he expanded his interest to cosmetic medicine and opened the MD Laser Skin Clinic located in Don Mills, of which he is the Medical Director. He is an Allergan certified injector for BOTOX COSMETIC®, and dermal fillers such as Juvederm and takes continuing education in the field of dermatology and aesthetic medicine.“Everyone wants to look good for their age, including me, which is why I think that there is a need and a place for aesthetic medicine to benefit our total health and well-being”.

Dr. Lam is married to Dr. Lo, (see her biography below) and has 2 children in university. During his spare time, he enjoys golf, tennis, and fixing things around the house.

dr. lily lo

Dr. Lo graduated from the University of Toronto, Faculty of Dentistry, in 1990, where she is currently a part-time clinical instructor in the Department of Oral Diagnosis and Medicine. She owns and works in two dental clinics located in Don Mills and Richmond Hill (her main office is right below the MD Laser Skin Clinic on the ground floor). Dr. Lo takes a special interest in cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, Invisalign® and in aesthetic medicine. She was certified in 2007 to perform cosmetic BOTOX® treatments and dermal fillers, under the delegation of Dr. Lam, M.D. Dr. Lo is also a provider of therapeutic BOTOX® for the treatment of TMJ-related muscle pain and headaches and can provide adjunctive treatments to alleviate symptoms of jaw pain. She has many years of experience not only as a cosmetic injector, but as a cosmetic dentist with orthodontic training, to improve facial aesthetics and harmony. “My passion is to give all my patients and clients not only a great smile, but a vibrant, youthful and energetic look that is possible these days, no matter the age... ”.

Dr. Lo is married and has 2 children in university. During her spare time, she enjoys tennis, tennis, and more tennis... plus some gardening.

eva ho

Eva began her aesthetics career in 1999, then obtained advanced aesthetics and medical aesthetics training and became a certified medical aesthetician in 2001. She worked as a medical Laser Technician in various cosmetic clinics and medical spa and in 2006, she was employed as a clinical doctor's assistant in a dermatologist clinic, performing all laser-related treatments to patients.

In 2013, Eva joined the MD Laser Skin Clinic as a medical laser technician, combining her skin aesthetics knowledge and her extensive medical laser experience to provide treatments for skin rejuvenation and permanent hair reduction. She is fluent in English, Cantonese, and Mandarin. Other than laser hair removal, Eva takes pride in providing many other aesthetic services including medical facials, chemical peeling, microdermabrasion, and IPL laser photorejuvenation.

In her spare time, Eva enjoys being a mom to her infant daughter, and well, there’s no time for anything else :)

olive amson

Olive Amson has been a certified aesthetician performing all aspects of beauty treatment since the early 1970's. In 2001, Olive received her training in laser aesthetics, primarily in laser hair removal and photo-rejuvenation. She worked as a laser technician for the next several years in various cosmetic surgical clinics owned by plastic surgeons and specialists. Her expertise led her to start a consulting business for setting up laser clinics for clients in the Greater Toronto area. In 2003, she started her own laser spa in Richmond Hill and now has a well-established laser clinic located at 16th Ave Dental & Laser Medi-spa. Olive currently offers her services as the Laser Director for the new Fractional Technology at MD Laser Skin Clinic, a laser spa that she helped set up in 2005. Welcome back, Olive!
